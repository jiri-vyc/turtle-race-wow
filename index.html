About the guild

We take our time to enjoy the game at our own pace. Lots of us have families, responsibilities, we cater to a more casual schedule. But we want to clear all the content, progressively, while tracking the "guild firsts" for each dungeon or raid on our website.

Want to join us?

Do you want to have a casual schedule but still not miss out on any content? You're our (wo)man! We are first and foremost a social guild. We think the core of the game is the community aspect and we try to create a family-like atmosphere in our guild by being social, helpful, creating custom guild events and just in general - have fun playing the game.

Also, we really love the lore of the game, so we want to clear all the content to progress the story and help our all members do it, beginners or veterans alike. You could call us semi-role-playing guild.

Current recruitment status

"Racing to the end. But slowly." The goal is not to be there first, but to clear all the content on the way and enjoy the road.
