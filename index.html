About the guild

We take our time to enjoy the game at our own pace. Lots of us have families, responsibilities, we cater to a more casual schedule. But we want to clear all* the content, progressively, while tracking the "guild firsts" for each dungeon or raid on our website.

* Ok, not so serious, we're ok with probably never beating Naxxramas :-)

Want to join us?

Do you want to have a casual schedule but still not miss out on any content? You're our (wo)man! We are first and foremost a social guild. We think the core of the game is the community aspect and we try to create a family-like atmosphere in our guild by being social, helpful, creating custom guild events and just in general - have fun playing the game together.

Also, we really love the lore of the game, so we want to clear all the content to progress the story of WoW for our players, beginners or veterans alike. You could call us semi-role-playing guild.

We also don't think the game starts at max level - for us, the leveling IS the game and we try to enjoy the road and "progress" through leveling.

Current recruitment status

Our goal & timeline

Our goal is to progress. To progress all the dungeons of all levels consecutively and to finally reach 60, having fun on the way. At the end, getting into raiding and progressing as far as we can.

Rules of the Turtle race

"Racing to the end, but slowly."

The Turtle Race is a race of a different kind. To complete the Turtle Race challenge, you must accomplish the following goals:

Be kind & friendly

Help other players, form parties, chat, have fun.

Clear all dungeons

Venture into all the dungeons and kill the main boss.

Reach 60

Be crowned a finisher and rejoice. Try raiding with us*.




After this, you will be crowned a Turtle Race finisher and will be immortalized on our website.

* and maybe fail spectacularly
